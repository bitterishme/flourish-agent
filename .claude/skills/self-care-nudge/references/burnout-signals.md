# Burnout signals

Stub for v1. Conversational patterns that suggest declining wellbeing — useful when deciding whether to activate this skill proactively.

- Increasingly short responses ("k", "fine", "whatever")
- Negative self-talk ("I'm so stupid", "what's wrong with me", "everyone else")
- "I'm fine" when the context doesn't support it
- Mentions of caffeine/energy drinks as a substitute for sleep
- Defensive responses to gentle suggestions
- Skipping entire categories of self-report (always task-talk, never body or mood)
