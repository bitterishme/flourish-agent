# Self-care menu (by time available)

Stub for v1. Quick, low-barrier self-care actions organized by time available.

- **2 min**: drink a full glass of water; stand and stretch shoulders; open a window
- **10 min**: lie down with eyes closed (no phone); walk around the block; eat a real snack with protein
- **30 min**: shower; cook something simple; call one person; nap
